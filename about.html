About me

Vanessa Tran

Hi! I'm a 2nd year Computational Media major at Georgia Institute of Technology with a focus in Intelligence and Interaction Design. I have a strong interest in artificial intelligence and its impact in UX design.

In my studies, I have learned that artificial intelligence, or machine learning, and UX designers have similar functions. They gather data and analyze users' interactions. Being able to use AI to improve the UX design process and enhance user experience has drawn my interest towards the combination of AI with interaction design.

What I study is a small part of who I am. Some things I love to do are reading books, trying out new restaurants, and kayaking! :)
